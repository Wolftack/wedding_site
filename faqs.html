Will there be vegan / vegetarian / gluten-free food options?

Yes of course. When you RSVP there will be space to indicate your dietary restrictions. Please also feel free to reach out to Luba and Tackin directly so we can plan accordingly.

Yes, we will have transportation arranged for guests from center of Saint-Émilion to our wedding venue. Exact location for pick up will be shared closer to the wedding.
