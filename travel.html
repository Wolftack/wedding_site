Travel to Saint-Émilion

Below you’ll find details about travel and hotel recommendations.

Getting there

Wedding celebrations will take place at Chateau Hotel Grand Barrail

3343 Route de Libourne, 33330 Saint-Émilion, France

From Bordeaux

Saint-Émilion is located a short drive or uber away from the center of Bordeaux (~45 min).

Alternatively, a quick regional TER train runs regularly from Bordeaux Saint-Jean (~35 min).

From Paris

The most convenient way is to take a TGV train from Paris to Bordeaux (~2–3 hours).

Then follow recommendations above on travel from Bordeaux to Saint-Émilion.

From Bilbao

Newark airport offers direct flights to Bilbao, making it an alternative option, especially for those interested in exploring the Basque country.

The most convenient way to get to Saint-Émilion is by driving (~4 hours).
